Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about maintenance

How often should solar panels be cleaned? ‚ñº
We recommend cleaning your solar panels 2-4 times per year depending on your location. Areas with high dust, pollen, or bird activity may require more frequent cleaning.
What happens if I don't maintain my system? ‚ñº
Without regular maintenance, solar panels can lose 15-25% efficiency due to dirt buildup. Additionally, undetected issues can lead to expensive repairs or system failures.
Can I perform maintenance myself? ‚ñº
While basic visual inspections are fine, professional maintenance is recommended. Climbing on roofs is dangerous, and improper cleaning or repairs can damage panels or void warranties.
Is monitoring included in all plans? ‚ñº
Basic monitoring is available for all customers. Premium and Enterprise plans include advanced monitoring features like real-time alerts and detailed analytics.
